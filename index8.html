Return Policies

At Raj Clothings, we strive to ensure that you are completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not happy with your order, we are here to help. Please read our return policy below for detailed information on how to process a return.

1. Eligibility for Returns

  • Time Frame: Items can be returned within 30 days from the date of purchase.
  • Condition: Items must be unused, unwashed, and in the original condition with all tags attached.
  • Receipt: A valid proof of purchase, such as a receipt or order confirmation email, is required for all returns.

2. Non-Returnable Items

  • Final Sale Items: Products marked as "Final Sale" cannot be returned or exchanged.
  • Intimate Apparel: For hygiene reasons, undergarments and swimwear are not eligible for return.
  • Customized Products: Personalized or custom-made items are non-returnable.

3. Return Process

Initiate Return:

  • Contact our Customer Service at [Customer Service Email] or [Customer Service Phone Number] to initiate your return.
  • Provide your order number and the reason for the return.

Prepare Your Package:

  • Securely pack the items in the original packaging, if possible.
  • Include your receipt or order confirmation email in the package.

Shipping the Return:

  • You are responsible for the cost of return shipping. We recommend using a trackable shipping service or purchasing shipping insurance.
  • Send the return to the following address:

4. Refunds

  • Processing Time: Once we receive your return, please allow 5-7 business days for processing.
  • Method of Refund: Refunds will be issued to the original method of payment.
  • Shipping Costs: Original shipping costs are non-refundable.

5. Exchanges

  • Availability: Exchanges are subject to product availability.
  • Process: To exchange an item, follow the return process above and indicate your desired replacement. If the replacement item is out of stock, we will process a refund instead.

6. Damaged or Defective Items

  • Inspection: If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact our Customer Service immediately.
  • Resolution: We will arrange for a replacement or refund for any defective or damaged items at no additional cost to you.

7. Store Credit

  • Option: You may opt for store credit instead of a refund.
  • Usage: Store credit will be issued in the form of a gift card and can be used for future purchases.
